MY JOURNEY INTO TECHNOLOGY

How it started

I am Zahraddeen Zakariyya, and from a very young age I have always been inspired to be a developer and a Sofyware Engineer.Technology has fascinated me for a long time ,not only because of the devices and tools we see everyday , but also because of the endless possibilities it creates for solving problems and making life easier.Even as a child I was curious about how Computers worked,how websites were built , and how applications come to life .So,that curiousity grew into Passion. One of the greatest sources of my inspiration has been my father Malam Zakariyya Ahmad. Although he is not in the field of technology himself, he has always played the role of a mentor to me. He would share technology videos with me, encourage me to learn, and remind me of the importance of hard work and persistence. His belief in me has shaped the way I see my journey—not just as a personal dream, but as a responsibility to use my skills to contribute positively to the world. Whenever I doubted myself, he motivated me to keep striving, reminding me that every expert once started as a beginner. Over the years, I have been putting in effort to learn and grow in this field. From taking the first steps in programming to exploring concepts in software engineering, I have been striving to build a strong foundation. I know the road is not easy, but I believe that with consistency, learning, and mentorship, I can achieve my goal. My vision is not only to become a skilled software engineer, but also to create solutions that will inspire others the same way I was inspired. This journey is still ongoing, but it has already taught me valuable lessons about discipline, patience, and passion. Technology, to me, is more than just a career choice—it is a calling that I am determined to follow. And with the guidance of my father’s mentorship and my own dedication, I am confident that I will continue to grow closer to my dream.
